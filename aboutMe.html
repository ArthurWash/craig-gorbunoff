About me.

I’m a broadcast technology expert and global sales engineer working at the intersection of creative production and cloud-based technology. I’ve partnered with Tier 1 broadcasters, major media organizations, and boutique production teams to modernize workflows, scale live content, and get real value from cloud and SaaS production solutions.

I began my career as an award-winning video producer, working across sports, fitness, and live digital media. That hands-on production background shapes how I approach technical problem-solving today. I understand the realities of live production, including tight timelines, lean crews, and high creative expectations, and I translate complex technology into solutions that work in practice.

In my current role, I support commercial and sales teams throughout the sales process, from discovery and solution design to technical demos and customer presentations. I help align customer needs with product capabilities, clearly articulate value to technical and non-technical stakeholders, and ensure proposed solutions are scalable, reliable, and grounded in real production workflows.

What sets me apart is my ability to bridge technical depth with creative understanding. I bring a production-first mindset to every engagement, helping teams move faster, sell smarter, and deliver better stories through technology.
